1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain functionality.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

  • Session cookies: Remember your session and keep you logged in
  • Security cookies: Protect against fraud and maintain security
  • Rate limiting cookies: Prevent spam and abuse

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience but are not essential for basic functionality.

  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Location cookies: Store your location preferences
  • Language cookies: Remember your language selection

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use our service so we can improve it.

  • Usage analytics: Track how you interact with our features
  • Performance monitoring: Monitor app performance and errors
  • A/B testing: Test different versions of features

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track marketing effectiveness.

  • Advertising cookies: Show you relevant ads based on your interests
  • Social media cookies: Enable sharing on social platforms
  • Third-party marketing: Allow partners to show you relevant ads

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third parties to place cookies on your device for:

  • Analytics and performance monitoring (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising and marketing (e.g., Google Ads, Facebook Pixel)
  • Social media integration (e.g., Facebook, Twitter)
  • Customer support tools (e.g., Intercom, Zendesk)

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies: Prevent all cookies from being stored
  • Block third-party cookies: Allow only our cookies
  • Clear existing cookies: Delete cookies already stored
  • Notify before accepting: Get prompted before cookies are set

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of specific types of tracking:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Google Ads: Ad Settings
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Preferences
  • NAI Opt-out: Network Advertising Initiative

5. Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Stored for up to 2 years
  • Analytics cookies: Stored for up to 26 months
  • Marketing cookies: Stored for up to 13 months
